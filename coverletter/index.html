I love that Bascamp sees value in diversity and what that can bring to software development and business. As part of the programming class that I teach I include a writing assignment where students are required to read an article on Diversity and Technology. They must then post their thoughts on a class forum, and also respond to at least one other of their peers posts. The interaction with the students is great, but I can tell many of the students have never thought about the fact that a field they hope to enter is pretty homogeneous. I also like that you value work/life balance. Co-workers who are only into coding are pretty boring, and not much fun to work with. But, well rounded employees are not just more interesting, they have a better perspective on their work add more value to the organization.
